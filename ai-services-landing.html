Questions

Things people ask first

Do I pay for the AI separately?
Token usage is small and billed at cost, or you plug in your own key. I set strict spending caps in the build, so there are no surprise bills.
How long does a build take?
A single assistant or automation is usually a few days. A connected system with integrations is one to two weeks. You get a clear timeline before we start.
What if it doesn't work for my business?
We start with a short scoping call and, where it makes sense, a small pilot — so you see it working on your real cases before committing to the full build.
Is my data safe?
Yes. API keys live server-side and are never exposed in the browser, and your data stays inside the tools you already use. Security is built in, not bolted on.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
No. I handle build, deployment and setup, then hand it over with a short walkthrough so your team can use it day one.
Which tools can you integrate with?
WordPress, Shopify, WhatsApp, CRMs, Google Sheets, email, couriers and most common business tools. Tell me your stack and I'll confirm.
